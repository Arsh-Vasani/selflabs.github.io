Discover the Power of Technology to Enhance your Lifestyle with SELF Labs

SELF Labs is a life science incubator that is dedicated to developing and investing in innovative products that leverage modern technology to improve and enrich people's lifestyles.

Our aim is to create entertaining, interactive solutions that not only provide enjoyment, but also address significant global challenges. Through our focus on leisure living, we strive to alleviate the difficulties and hardships that individuals face in their daily lives and bring enjoyment and fulfillment to people around the world.

17 Initiatives

Goal #1 Identify The Human Soul

Understanding the human soul is an important aspect of understanding the human experience and our place in the world. It can help us gain insight into our own motivations, desires, and beliefs, and can also help us connect with others and find meaning in life.

Goal #2 End Extreme World Hunger

Ending extreme world hunger is important because it is a fundamental human right for all people to have access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food. Hunger and malnutrition have devastating impacts on individuals, communities, and entire societies, and can lead to poverty, illness, and even death. By working to end extreme world hunger, we can help ensure that everyone has the opportunity to live healthy, productive lives.

Goal #3 Provide Sustainable Farming

Our exclusive game Cultivate Coin, is a Trivial Based Simulation game that teaches you how to run a Vertical Farm Business in three different perspectives Operator, Farmer, and Negotiator. The game is inspired and is connected to our Off-The Grid Growboxes in Real Life. As we grow produces and distribute Players are able to vote on real life operations on how we grow crops and distribute them. This will help Decentralize the Farming Industry and spread education of not only how to run the business but to grow crops like a pro.

Goal #4 End Extreme Poverty

Ending extreme poverty is important because it is a fundamental human right for all people to have the resources and opportunities they need to live dignified, fulfilling lives. Extreme poverty is characterized by an inability to meet basic needs, including food, shelter, and access to education and healthcare.

Goal #5 Provide Sustainable Living

Sustainable living is important because it is a way of meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It involves using natural resources in a responsible and efficient way, minimizing waste and pollution, and conserving the environment for the benefit of all. Adopting sustainable living practices can help reduce our impact on the planet, protect natural resources and biodiversity, and contribute to long-term social, economic, and environmental well-being. By working towards sustainable living, we can help ensure that the earth's resources are used wisely and sustainably, and that future generations have the opportunity to thrive.

Goal #6 Provide Universal Education Systems

Providing universal education systems is important because it is a fundamental human right for all people to have access to quality education. Education is a key factor in promoting personal and societal development, and is essential for the well-being of individuals, communities, and nations. It can help individuals acquire the knowledge, skills, and values they need to participate fully in society and to achieve their full potential. By providing universal education systems, we can help ensure that every person has the opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute to the world around them. This can lead to a more equitable and prosperous society for all.

Goal #7 Eradicate Forced Labar

Eradicating forced labor is important because it is a fundamental human right to be free from slavery and exploitation. Forced labor is a form of modern slavery that occurs when people are coerced, threatened, or deceived into working against their will, often for little or no pay. It can take many forms, including debt bondage, forced child labor, human trafficking, and other forms of exploitation. Forced labor is a serious human rights violation that affects millions of people around the world and can have devastating impacts on their lives and well-being. By working to eradicate forced labor, we can help ensure that all people are able to live free from slavery and exploitation, and are able to enjoy their fundamental rights and freedoms.

Goal #8 Provide Access To Affordable, Reliable, & Modern Energy

Providing sustainable energy is important because it is essential for powering our economies, communities, and daily lives. Sustainable energy refers to energy sources that are reliable, clean, and renewable, and that can meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to do the same. Sustainable energy sources include solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and bioenergy, and they offer a number of benefits compared to fossil fuels, such as reduced greenhouse gas emissions, improved air quality, and greater energy security. By working to provide sustainable energy, we can help reduce our impact on the environment, improve public health, and support long-term economic and social development.

Goal #9 Deploy Research Labs Around The World

Deploying research labs around the world is important because it can help promote scientific research and innovation on a global scale. Research labs are places where scientists and other researchers can conduct experiments, test hypotheses, and develop new ideas and technologies. By deploying research labs in different parts of the world, we can help ensure that scientists have access to the resources and facilities they need to conduct their work, and that research and innovation are not limited to certain regions or countries. This can help advance our understanding of the world and drive the development of new solutions to global challenges. Additionally, deploying research labs around the world can help promote collaboration and exchange between researchers from different cultural and scientific backgrounds, which can lead to new insights and innovations that might not have been possible otherwise.

Goal #10 Promote and Protect The Rights Of All People

Promoting and protecting the rights of all people is important because it is a fundamental human right to be treated with dignity and respect, and to have access to the opportunities and resources needed to live a fulfilling life. This includes the right to life, liberty, and security of person; the right to education, work, and health; the right to participate in cultural life and to express one's identity; and many other rights. By promoting and protecting the rights of all people, we can help ensure that everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute to the well-being of their communities. This can lead to a more equitable and just society for all.

Goal #11 Provide Affordable Health Care Systems

Providing affordable healthcare systems is important because it is a fundamental human right to have access to quality healthcare. Healthcare is essential for the well-being of individuals, communities, and nations, and it can help prevent illness, reduce suffering, and improve quality of life. However, many people around the world struggle to access healthcare due to a lack of financial resources, geographic barriers, or other factors. By working to provide affordable healthcare systems, we can help ensure that everyone has the opportunity to receive the healthcare they need, regardless of their ability to pay. This can lead to a healthier, more productive society for all.

Goal #12 Protect and Promote High Standard Animal Health

Protecting and promoting high standard animal health is important because it is essential for the well-being of animals and for the sustainability of our planet. Animals play a vital role in many ecosystems and are a vital source of food, fiber, and other resources for humans. Ensuring that animals are healthy and well-cared for can help protect them from suffering and promote their well-being, and it can also have positive impacts on human health and the environment. By working to protect and promote high standard animal health, we can help ensure that animals are treated with care and respect, and that their contributions to our lives and the planet are valued and preserved.

Goal #13 Free People From 9-5 Work Schedules

Freeing people from 9-5 work schedules is important because it can help improve work-life balance and enhance well-being. The traditional 9-5 work schedule can be inflexible and can make it difficult for people to attend to their personal and family responsibilities, or to pursue other interests and activities outside of work. By working to free people from 9-5 work schedules, we can help create more flexible and accommodating work environments that allow people to balance their work and personal lives more effectively. This can lead to increased productivity, satisfaction, and well-being for both individuals and organizations. It can also help to promote social and economic equity by allowing more people to have access to paid work, regardless of their availability during standard working hours.

Goal #15 Scientifically Prove Astrology To Be True Or False

It has not been possible to scientifically prove astrology to be true or false because astrology has not been adopted as a scientific concept. Astrology is a system of belief that holds that the positions and movements of celestial bodies, such as the sun, moon, and planets, can influence human affairs and natural phenomena. With the enhancement of technology the Goal is to use prove the movements of the planets effecting Human Life.

Goal #16 Help The Public Set A Global Standard For Business & Personal Ethics

Helping the public set a global standard for business and personal ethics is important because ethical behavior is essential for building trust, respect, and confidence in society. Ethical conduct involves acting in accordance with principles of honesty, fairness, responsibility, and respect for others. Setting a global standard for business and personal ethics can help ensure that people are held to the same high standards of ethical behavior, regardless of where they live or work. This can help promote a more just and equitable society, and can also help build trust and confidence in business and personal relationships. There are many ways that the public can help set a global standard for business and personal ethics, including participating in community initiatives and advocacy groups, supporting companies and individuals that adhere to high ethical standards, and holding others accountable for their actions.

Goal #17 Enhance Human Consciousness

Enhancing human consciousness is a complex and multifaceted goal that could have many potential benefits for individuals and society. Human consciousness refers to the subjective experience of being aware of one's own thoughts, feelings, and surroundings. It is a fundamental aspect of the human experience and is influenced by a variety of factors, including genetics, environment, and experiences. Enhancing human consciousness could potentially involve a range of approaches, such as improving mental and emotional well- being, developing greater self-awareness, expanding one's understanding of the world and others, and cultivating more positive and meaningful relationships. By enhancing human consciousness, we may be able to improve quality of life and contribute to a more compassionate and understanding society.
